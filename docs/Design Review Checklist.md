Use this checklist during design reviews. Each item requires a response and assignee.

Architecture: Does the architecture meet functional and non-functional requirements?
Interfaces: Are APIs and event formats defined and versioned?
Data Flow: Are all sensitive data flows identified and secured?
Smart Contracts: Are events, gas costs, and access control modeled?
DB Schema: Is normalization adequate? Are indices defined?
Error handling: Are retries and idempotency covered?
Security: Are threat models and mitigations included?
Scalability: Are scaling points and limits defined?
Observability: Are metrics, traces, and logs defined?
Compliance: Are data retention and privacy constraints addressed?
Testing: Are test cases and coverage goals defined?
Operational: Are deployment & rollback plans defined?
Documentation: Is SDD complete and RTM up-to-date?
